Regularization Letter

Stark, Tony

To: Potts, Pepper

August 25, 2023

Tony Stark

Malibu, California


Dear Pepper,


We are pleased to inform you that because you met all the requirements and girlfriend standards, you will be regularized on August 28, 2023.


With that, your probationary period for the girlfriend status with your genius, billionaire, playboy, philanthropist[1] boyfriend will now end. And you will now be a full-time girlfriend.


But now you would be asking, what would change after regularization? Nothing really, it just means I really appreciate everything you do for us. You always do your best to understand where I’m coming from. Thinking about it now, it’s amazing how you’re able to put up with my naivety. For the patience and effort that you pour into this relationship to work, even though we have the biggest differences sometimes. I love how we compromise for things to work for the both of us, I really love you for that.


I know I’m not the best there is, I lack a lot of things. But you are my little gem that holds glimmer of inspiration for me to always try my best. I want us to grow together and support each other. I can’t express with words how much I admire you. I’m really glad I found a gem that’s as sublime as you are.


I adore you, my dear Pepper.

Happy 1st Anniversary!



I would also like to cordially invite you to a date around Manila.
